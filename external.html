Ilahia College Of Arts And Science

Ilahia College of Arts and Science, located in Muvattupuzha, Ernakulam, Kerala state, India, is an arts and science college. The campus is situated 5 km from the city, at top of a hill ICAS was established in 1995. It is approved by the AICTE and affiliated to the Mahatma Gandhi University.
